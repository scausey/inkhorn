I read past my bedtime, mainly a diet of young adult science fiction and fantasy. Like a lot of readers, I'm working on a handful of stories I want to throw out into the world that I hope at least one person will like.

Margaret Atwood signed my copy of A Handmaid's Tale (which I have a picture of but can't find at the moment!) at BookCon 2017 and I just about died right then and there. I was obsessed with Sarah J. Maas for a moment, but the craze has since then died down. My favorite series at the moment is The Lunar Chronicles by Marissa Meyer. My best friend and I dress up as literary characters sometimes ("Can ya hear the empty spaces?!").

When I'm not reading, I try to build things like this website and learn more about CSS animations and JavaScript. Python is love. If you like code too, check out my github!
